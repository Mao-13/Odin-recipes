Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of Italy's most iconic dishes, known for its crispy base and versatility. It can be customized with a variety of toppings, from tomato sauce and mozzarella to any desired ingredients. Perfect for sharing at a party or enjoying on a movie night.

Ingredients

  • 500 g (about 4 cups) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 7 g (about 2 teaspoons) dry yeast
  • 400 g (about 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 300 g (about 10 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Ingredients like pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Assemble the pizza
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly
  5. Slice and enjoy
