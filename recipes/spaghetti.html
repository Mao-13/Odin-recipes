Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a type of long, thin pasta that is a classic in Italian cuisine. Combined with various sauces, especially tomato-based ones, it becomes a simple yet delicious dish, ideal for any occasion. It's perfect as a quick meal or as a comforting option for a dinner at home.

Ingredients

  • 400 g (about 14 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400 g (about 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until golden
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the sauce and mix well
  5. Serve hot, garnishing with fresh basil and Parmesan if desired
