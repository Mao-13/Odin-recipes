Lasagna



Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made up of layers of pasta, meat, tomato sauce, and a blend of cheeses. Its rich and creamy texture, combined with the intense flavors of the sauce and meat, makes it a favorite for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • sauce
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or regular)
  • 400 g (about 14 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 300 g (about 10 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 100 g (about 3.5 oz) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture
  3. Assemble the lasagna
  4. Bake
  5. Cool, slice, and serve
